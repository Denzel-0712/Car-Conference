Conference Overview

Where Consignors and Auctions Connect

Since 1995, The Conference of Automotive Remarketing (CAR) has served as the hub for the remarketing community, with more consignors and auctions in attendance than any other event of its kind. CAR is an in-person event designed strategically for connecting and educating the automotive remarketing industry. The event serves as the venue for the industry to decide on initiatives and establish partnerships. This conference explores the future of the automatics industry.
